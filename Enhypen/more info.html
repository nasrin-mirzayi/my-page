Name

The name Enhypen was introduced during the live broadcast of the final episode of I-LAND, the group derives their name from the hypen symbol (-), representing "connection,discovery & growth.
similar to how a hypen connect different words to create new meanings, Enhypen aims "to come together to connect, discover and grow together to form a new act".
